Fear of crime significantly impacts people’s daily activities, restricting pedestrian mobility, limiting social interactions, and affecting overall well-being. Research suggests that individuals who feel unsafe while walking in urban areas often alter their behavior by avoiding certain routes, reducing outdoor activity after dark, or relying on alternative transport methods.

How StreetWise Helps Make Streets Safer for Everyone

To address these challenges, we developed StreetWise—a mobile-first, community-sourced maps application. It provides real-time safety information, helping users select the safest walking routes based on incident reports.

Green: Safe Route

Indicates a safe route with minimal safety concerns.

Yellow: Moderate Risk

Highlights routes with some safety concerns that users should be aware of.

Red: High-Risk Areas

Identifies high-risk areas where users should exercise caution.

How It Works

Report Incidents

Contribute to a safer community by reporting incidents such as poor lighting, obstacles, or harassment.

Dynamic Route Suggestions

Our system suggests the safest walking routes using a color-coded system to represent real-time safety levels.

Real-time Updates

Get live updates on safety conditions, ensuring you always have the latest information to stay safe.
