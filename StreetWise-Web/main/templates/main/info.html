About StreetWise

Enhancing pedestrian safety through real-time community collaboration.

Our Mission

StreetWise is dedicated to enhancing pedestrian safety in our community. We empower users with real-time safety information so they can make informed decisions about their walking routes.

How It Works

Report Incidents

Contribute to a safer community by reporting incidents such as poor lighting, obstacles, or harassment to build a comprehensive safety map.

Dynamic Route Suggestions

Our system suggests the safest walking routes using a color-coded system that reflects real-time safety levels.

Real-time Updates

Receive live updates on safety conditions, ensuring you always have the latest information to stay safe.

Data Analysis

Incident Type Distribution

Calendar Heatmap
